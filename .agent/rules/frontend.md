### Visual Design & Aesthetics
* **Premium Feel:** Apply subtle noise textures to main backgrounds for a tactile feel.
* **Depth & Shadows:** Use multi-layered drop shadows to make cards look "lifted". Interactive elements should use colored shadows for a "glow" effect.
* **Responsive Layouts:** Use `LayoutBuilder` and `MediaQuery`. Ensure the app adapts perfectly to Mobile and Web.
* **Iconography:** Incorporate icons liberally to enhance logical navigation. Use `ImageIcon` for custom assets.
