Delete Your Account

Eyesea Reporting

⚠️ Warning: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including your reports, profile information, and account settings. This action cannot be reversed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All pollution reports you have submitted
  • All photos and images associated with your reports
  • Your location data and preferences
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Your authentication credentials

What Will Be Retained

  • Aggregated, anonymized data used for statistical purposes
  • Data required by law to be retained (if applicable)

How to Request Account Deletion

Method 1: Through the App (Recommended)

If you have the Eyesea Reporting app installed:

  1. Open the Eyesea Reporting app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions

Method 2: By Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email request:

  1. Send an email to privacy@eyesea.org
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be marked for deletion immediately
  • Data will be permanently removed within 30 days of your request
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Need Help?
