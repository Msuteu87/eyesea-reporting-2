# Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 (Beta)

**First Release - Help protect our oceans!**

### Key Features
• **AI-Powered Detection** - Automatically identifies pollution in photos using on-device AI
• **Map View** - See pollution reports on an interactive map
• **Offline Support** - Report pollution even without internet - syncs when connected
• **Social Feed** - View and engage with community reports
• **Cleanup Events** - Join organized cleanup activities
• **Leaderboard** - Track your environmental impact

### How It Works
1. Capture a photo of pollution
2. AI analyzes and categorizes it
3. Add location and details
4. Submit to help protect marine environments

**Privacy-first:** All AI processing happens on your device. Your data is encrypted and secure.
